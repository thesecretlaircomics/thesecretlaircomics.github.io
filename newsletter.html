Happy Birthday!

It's a BIG month for birthdays, America is turning 250, and we're turning 12! Ah to be twelve again...the Chicago Bulls with Michael Jordan, Nintendo 64, Independence Day, and I had two legs still! But times change and as we age, we grow.

Recently we hired a new store manager (Traci) but after a couple weeks Traci is stepping down to a store employee role and we've hired Puck (a local to the Magic scene) to replace them as store manager. Puck has a lot of gaming experience and is in the process to become a Magic judge, whereas they have little comic knowledge but are eager to learn that side of the store. Benjamin is also leaving us after 3yrs and that will be a tough blow to the store. Benjamin is a very reliable employee and an easy Mount Rushmore of employee candidate for the shop! But maybe teaching and he won't work out and he'll come back (I kid, I hope he does well and I'm sure he will!). However I am very confident in the staff we have/will gain and hope things will get back to a more "normal" pace.

As for a recap of last month comic wise, the big book was still Absolute Batman but Absolute Green Arrow has all the buzz currently. Corpse Knight and Dorc are still doing big numbers for indie books and as for Marvel, Avengers Armageddon wasn't a bad read and you got a free Magic card which definitely helped boost sales. I'm working on a couple creator visits for later in the summer/fall so keep an eye out for those.

Gaming wise we ran several events last month all of which went over very well and this month's events are going to be some of our biggest gaming events to date! It was very cool to run the Magic Pre-Release events last month and it was cool to see brand new players doing well and all new winners! The store has always been about community and our gaming community is really becoming as strong as our comic community!

We also raised $400.00 for the RHSPCA via the Cap bucket, so that spare change made an impact! So thank you to all that donated!

In closing we just wanted to say thank you to everyone who has ever been to the shop! We hope you enjoyed your visit and we look forward to continuing trying to provide the best comic/gaming experience we can!
